Title
# Voice of Customer Analytics Platform
Project Overview:
This project analyzes Google Reviews from leading interior design brands including Minimalix, Livspace, and HomeLane to uncover customer satisfaction drivers, recurring pain points, competitive strengths, and actionable business opportunities.
The solution combines NLP, sentiment analysis, TF-IDF, BERTopic, root cause analysis, and interactive Power BI dashboards.

Business Problem: 
Ratings alone do not explain why customers are satisfied or dissatisfied.

This project answers:

- Why customers leave positive or negative reviews
- What recurring issues appear across competitors
- Which companies perform better on specific dimensions
- What operational improvements can increase satisfaction

Dataset:
- 999 Google Reviews collected
- 546 textual reviews analyzed
- Companies:
  - Minimalix
  - HomeLane
  - Livspace
 
Tech Stack:

### Python
- Pandas
- NumPy

### NLP
- NLTK
- TF-IDF
- BERTopic
- VADER Sentiment Analysis

### Visualization
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Power BI


Methodology:
1. Data Collection
2. Data Cleaning
3. Exploratory Data Analysis
4. Sentiment Analysis
5. TF-IDF Keyword Extraction
6. Topic Modeling using BERTopic
7. Competitor Benchmarking
8. Root Cause Analysis
9. Business Recommendations
10. Power BI Dashboarding


Key Findings:
### HomeLane
- Customers primarily discuss project execution and delivery timelines.
- Negative reviews are dominated by execution delays.

### Livspace
- Strong customer experience scores.
- Complaints focus on post-sales support and service consistency.

### Minimalix
- Reviews heavily focus on lead quality, subscription ROI, and partner support.
- Business model differs significantly from traditional interior-design competitors.

### Overall Insight
Customers care more about service experience, communication, and execution quality than design aesthetics alone.
